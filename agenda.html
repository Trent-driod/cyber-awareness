Workshop Agenda & Extended Learning Path

This agenda includes pre-event preparation, two workshop days, and post-event follow-up for long-term impact tracking.

Pre-Workshop Assignment (Week 1)

  • Complete pre-awareness survey (baseline knowledge, transaction volumes, prior fraud experiences).
  • Collect MoMo and merchant record examples for group case studies.
  • Read one-month fraud report summary from NCA and West African regional alerts.

Day 1 (4 hours)

  1. Welcome, introductions and objectives (10 mins).
  2. Cybersecurity essentials: confidentiality, integrity, availability (30 mins).
  3. MoMo fraud incident flow and real case examples (45 mins).
  4. Short break and networking (15 mins).
  5. Role-play: identifying scam phone scripts (30 mins).
  6. Workshop task: build a security checklist for a trading kiosk (55 mins).

Day 2 (4 hours)

  1. Review Day 1 findings and successes (15 mins).
  2. Fake job recruitment scam anatomy, proof-checking evidence (35 mins).
  3. Interactive group mapping of attacker routes (20 mins).
  4. Break (10 mins).
  5. Social media and online payment safety practices (50 mins).
  6. Scenario simulation game, feedback and action planning (60 mins).
  7. Closing summary and accountability plan (30 mins).

Post-Workshop Follow-up (Month 1)

  • Collect post-assessment scores and participant satisfaction data.
  • Perform a second survey to measure behavior change and incident reduction.
  • Provide weekly email tips and a report template for fraud tracking.

Deliverables: printed toolkit, digital resources, risk register, and a practical action plan for each implementation team.
