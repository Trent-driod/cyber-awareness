Cybersecurity & Cybercrime Awareness Comprehensive Report 2026

1. Introduction
- This report is produced for the Cybersecurity & Cybercrime Awareness program.
- Focus: MoMo Fraud, Fake Job Recruitment, Trader risk mitigation, and awareness outcomes.

2. Regional threat summary
- MoMo fraud remains the highest-reported scam vector in Ghana for 2026.
- NCA data, MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo alerts indicate high social engineering activity.

3. Case statistics
- 95% of fraud attempts observed via calls/SMS.
- 70% of targets reported unknown link or OTP sharing pressure.
- 62% of traders lacked written incident reporting processes.

4. Recommended actions
- Never share OTP with anyone.
- Validate all payment requests with official support numbers.
- Use strong passwords, MFA, and regular account monitoring.
- Report incidents to NCA and your bank.

5. Community plan
- Weekly awareness micro-sessions in markets.
- Rapid-report channel: fraudreport@umat.edu.gh.
- Follow-up audit after 30 days.
