Ghana MoMo Fraud (2023-2026) - Local Mobile Money Scams

Criminals pose as MTN/Vodafone agents, claiming account issues and requesting OTPs via SMS/calls. Victims lose GHS 500-5000 instantly.

Impact: NCA reports 100k+ cases yearly; traders lose daily revenue.
  • Never share OTP—banks never ask.
  • Verify via official app only.
  • Report to 111 immediately.
Twitter Bitcoin Scam (2020) - High-Profile Phishing

Hackers compromised verified accounts (Obama, Musk) to promote BTC giveaway scam. $120k stolen in hours.

Impact: Eroded platform trust; users lost $100-10k each.
  • Enable 2FA everywhere.
  • Suspicious links? Check URL manually.
  • "Too good to be true" = scam.
Carbanak Gang (2013-2018) - Banking Trojan Heist

Malware infected bank employee PCs, allowing $1B theft via fake transfers/video manipulation.

Impact: 30+ banks hit; avg $10M loss per target.
  • Use antivirus + email filters.
  • Verify large transfers in person.
  • Train staff on phishing.
Equifax Breach (2017) - Data Exposure

Hackers stole 147M personal records (SSN, cards) due to unpatched software.

Impact: $700M fines; identity theft for years.
  • Patch software weekly.
  • Freeze credit reports.
  • Monitor accounts daily.
Fake Job Scams (Ongoing) - Trader Targeting

Scammers offer "remote trader jobs" requiring upfront fees or wallet setup via fake sites.

Impact: Ghana traders lose GHS 1000-10k upfront.
  • No legit job asks for payment first.
  • Research company on official site.
  • Use reverse image search on "job ads".
