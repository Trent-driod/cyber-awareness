Real-World Cyber Scenarios & Defense Guide

🚨 MoMo Fraud - Fake Reversal Call

Scenario: "Your agent reversal failed, send OTP to fix"

Scammer calls pretending to be MTN/Vodafone agent. Claims transaction reversal error, asks for phone number or OTP to "complete refund".

Real Impact: ₵2,300 average loss per incident. 95% of MoMo fraud uses social engineering.
  • Real agents NEVER ask for OTP over phone
  • Check balance/log in app directly before responding
  • Save official helplines: MTN 100, Vodafone 111

Call 111 NCA now if targeted

📧 Phishing - Fake Job Offer

Scenario: "Job offer - pay ₵200 processing fee via MoMo"

WhatsApp message offers overseas job. Asks for "registration fee" or bank details to "process documents". Links to fake site stealing info.

Real Impact: Ghana recorded 14,000+ job scam complaints in 2025.
  • No legitimate job asks for payment upfront
  • Verify company on official website (not WhatsApp link)
  • Enable 2FA on WhatsApp/Email before sharing

Action: Forward scam message to NCA (111 or report@nca.gov.gh)

⚠️ Incident Response - What To Do NOW

Immediate Steps If Compromised

Your account shows unauthorized transactions or suspicious login.

48-Hour Window: Quick action recovers 62% of funds vs 12% after delay.
  • Freeze account: MTN *170# > 0 > 4, Vodafone *110# > 8
  • Report to bank/telco + NCA 111 within 1 hour
  • Change passwords from SAFE device
  • Scan phone: Download official antivirus
